1. Introduction

Welcome to Geom Compass ("we," "us," "our," or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

WE DO NOT COLLECT, STORE, OR TRANSMIT ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location history or tracking data
  • Contact lists or address book information
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

3. Information Used Locally

3.1 Location Information

The App accesses your device's GPS functionality solely for:

  • Calculating magnetic declination based on geographic location
  • Displaying your position on maps
  • Providing location-based compass functionality
Important: Location data is processed entirely on your device and is not stored, transmitted, or retained after the App is closed.

3.2 Device Sensors

The App uses your device's sensors (magnetometer, GPS, accelerometer) in real-time. All sensor data is processed locally and is never stored or transmitted.

3.3 User Preferences

The following preferences are stored locally on your device:

  • True North/Magnetic North display preference
  • Display settings and UI preferences

These are removed when you delete the App.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenStreetMap

The App displays map data from OpenStreetMap (OSM). When using map features:

  • Map tile requests are sent directly to OSM servers
  • Your IP address may be logged by OSM per their privacy policy

OSM Privacy Policy: https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

4.2 No Other Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK or social media trackers
  • Advertising networks
  • External crash reporting services

5. Data Security

All data processing occurs exclusively on your device. We implement standard iOS/Android security protections for local storage.

6. Your Rights

  • Location Permissions: You may disable location access in device settings
  • Data Deletion: Delete the App to remove all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Compliance

We comply with:

  • GDPR: No PII collected; data processed only on device
  • CCPA: No personal information sold or shared

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
